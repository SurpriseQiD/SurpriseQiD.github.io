关于 QiView

QiView 是一个面向计算与决策科学的知识分享与整理平台，覆盖博弈论、机器学习、深度学习、大语言模型等主题。网站保持学术化、专业化的写作风格，兼顾可复现性与实用性。

本站定位

  • 聚焦核心原理与可落地的方法论
  • 以中文为主，必要时补充英文原文链接/术语
  • 优先提供数据、代码要点或参考资料的出处

使用与联系

  • 如需纠错、补充或合作，请通过站内评论或公开联系方式留言
  • 站点不展示个人隐私信息，所有内容以 “QiView” 名义发布

About QiView

QiView is a knowledge hub for computation and decision sciences (game theory, machine learning, deep learning, large language models). Content stays academic, reproducible, and practical.

Positioning

  • Focus on core principles and applicable methods
  • Primarily in Chinese; English terminology and references are added when helpful
  • Cite sources and share data/code pointers when possible

Contact & contributions

  • For corrections, additions, or collaboration, please comment on-site or use the public contact channels
  • Personal details are omitted; all posts are published under the “QiView” name
